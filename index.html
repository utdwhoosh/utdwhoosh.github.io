Say Hello To Whoosh

Taking student accessibility to go,
here at The University of Texas at Dallas.

From maps to contacts, and more, right on your phone.

Learn More Get it on Github

Whoosh makes Campus Accessibility accessible

Whoosh uses Google Maps and data provided by the Office of Student AccessAbility to bring campus accessibility to Android in a whole new way. Everything from finding accessible routes to accessibility points of contact at a tap, with more to come.

Features Get it on Github
Campus Maps

With interactive accessibility routes, floor-by-floor layouts, and accessibility devices in every building, Whoosh makes the map more than a map.

Points of Contact

With points of contact at hand for the Office of Student AccessAbility, campus contacts, and more, you'll always be moving in the right direction.

Clean & Intuitive

Designed with humans in mind, every part of the app works together for a seamless experience.
